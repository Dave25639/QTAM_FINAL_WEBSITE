Our Mission

The mission of QuantA&M is to connect students with the resources they need to learn more about the development, implementation, and application of quantum computing technology. The organization also hopes to bring a new generation of inquisitive students to the forefront of quantum computing innovation as we enter a society which uses quantum computing as a mainstream method of solving complex problems.
